About Us

A professionally managed organization and is an outcome of over 30 years of combined experience in implementation of various projects and design integration. We are a group of environmental engineers working to provide solutions to the environmental problems.

Anu Enviro Consultants

We are a group of environmental engineers working to provide solutions to the environmental problems. AEC specializes in multidisciplinary fields such as water & waste-water treatment, zero liquid discharge, building services like watershed management, fire auditing etc. AEC deals in both designing & execution of waste-water treatment plants as well as turnkey plants. AEC has a energetic staff including both young and senior professionals having ability to combine advanced technologies with simple solutions results in improvement and benefit to the end user.
