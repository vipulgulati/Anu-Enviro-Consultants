About Us

We Are
Environmental Heroes

A professionally managed organization and is an outcome of over 30 years of combined experience in implementation of various projects and design integration. AEC specializes in multidisciplinary fields such as water and waste-water treatment, zero liquid discharge, building services like watershed management, fire auditing and other environmental consultation. AEC deals in both designing and execution of waste-water treatment plants as well as turnkey plants.

Has an energetic staff including both young and senior professionals having ability to combine advanced technologies with simple solutions results in improvement and benefit to the end user. Through our motivated and dedicated team of well qualified and experienced professionals we have come out to offer services which are completely and comprehensive in the field of Project Management.
