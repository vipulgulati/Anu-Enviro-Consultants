About Us

We Are
Environmental Heroes

We are a group of environmental engineers working to provide solutions to the environmental problems. AEC specializes in multidisciplinary fields such as water & waste-water treatment, zero liquid discharge, building services like watershed management, fire auditing etc.

AEC deals in both designing & execution of waste-water treatment plants as well as turnkey plants. AEC has a energetic staff including both young and senior professionals having ability to combine advanced technologies with simple solutions results in improvement and benefit to the end user.

Tube Settler Media

Our Tube Settler media provides a large settling surface area within the specified volume thus giving high flow rates.

MBBR Media

This specially designed floating MBBR media provides large surface area for bacterial growth.
These carrier elements (media) are in continuous movement, available surface area is constantly exposed to the wastewater, and hence uniform biological growth takes place over entire area of the media.

SAFF Media

For higher BOD, where the risk of clogging of media may be higher, SAFF media is recommended. This SAFF media consists of a hybrid of low flute and high flute cross corrugated sheets thereby keeping surface area relatively high but also increasing the module openings.

Effective Microorganism

When EM is introduced to a polluted area like wastewater, Solid waste and soil, the EM microorganism will immediately begin feeding on the organic matter and multiplying – creating in the process, enzymes that curb pollution.
